### Game Format
- **Quarters:** 10 minutes running time
- **Stop Time:** Last 2 minutes of 4th quarter and OT
- **Defense:** Half-court only (no press)
- **Zone:** Not allowed (man-to-man only)
- **Halftime:** 5 minutes
