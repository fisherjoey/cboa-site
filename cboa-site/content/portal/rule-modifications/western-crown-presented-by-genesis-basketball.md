### Game Format
- **Quarters:** 9 minutes stop time
- **Mercy:** 25+ points in 4th = running time
- **Press:** No restrictions

### U13 Division
- **Half Court Zone:** Not allowed
- **Full Court Press:** Allowed
- **Press Mercy:** 30 points
