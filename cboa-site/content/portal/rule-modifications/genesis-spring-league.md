### Game Format
- **Quarters:** 9 minutes stop time
- **Mercy Rule:** None
- **Press Rules:** None
- **3-Point Line:** Outside line if two marked
- **Timeouts:** FIBA (2 and 3)
- **Overtime:** 2 minutes, 1 timeout each
- **Shot Clock:** 14 seconds on offensive rebound
- **Halftime:** 5 minutes
