  # Welcome to CBOA

  The Calgary Basketball Officials Association (CBOA) has been serving the Calgary basketball community for over 60 years. We are dedicated to providing qualified, professional basketball officials for all levels of play in the Calgary area.

  ## Why Choose CBOA?

  Our officials are trained to the highest standards, ensuring fair and professional officiating at every level of competition. Whether you're organizing a youth league, high school tournament, or competitive adult league, CBOA provides experienced officials who understand the game.

  ## Join Our Team

  Interested in becoming a basketball official? CBOA offers comprehensive training programs for new officials and ongoing development opportunities for experienced referees. Join our community of dedicated professionals who share a passion for the game.
